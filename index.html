About Me

Serena Chan

I am a first generation, third year student at the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Economics with a minor in Data Science. I have a passion for marketing, strategy, data analytics, graphic design, client service, and product management. I was raised in San Francisco, currently based in Berkeley, and aspire to live in New York City.

My personal values place people at the center of my work which is why I focus on creating eye-catching content to meet a large target audience. Driven by my imagination and ambition, I constantly set new goals for myself and through my curiousity, I am able to discover what interests me. I enjoy meeting new people and maintaining meaningful relationships as each person teaches me something new and exciting.

I embrace diversity and change, allowing me to thrive in a fast-paced environment that constantly adapts with the development of new technology. My versatility and determination help me navigate difficult challenges that allow me to become a stronger individual, positively influencing and transforming any project or task that I partake in. With my adventurous spirit and interest in business and technology, I hope to make impact wherever I go. I welcome opportunities to apply my more creative skills in business, such as developing strategies and materials while relating it to big data.

My hobbies include: attending Broadway Musicals and symphonies, kayaking, bouldering, graphic and web design, and going on food crawls around the Bay Area.
